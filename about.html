Fresh Juice, Healthy Roots About Our Shop

Founded in 2019 by the Vivek Bharti, our establishment has been serving up fresh fruits sourced from artisan farmers in various regions of India. We are dedicated to travelling India, finding the best quality fruits, and bringing back to you as juice here in our shop.

We guarantee that you will fall in love with our decadent blends the moment you walk inside until you finish your last sip. Join us for your daily routine, an outing with friends, or simply just to enjoy some alone time.
